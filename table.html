ABOUT ALTSCHOOL AFRICA

ALtSchool Africa is an innovative online learning platform designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the tech industry. It offers specialized programs in Software Engineering, Product Management, and Data Science, providing hands-on experience and mentorship from industry experts.
